Elemental Exemplars

Elemental Exemplars

Summary

Race to the end with a friend and your followers! The followers are the key to this puzzle, but beware! Fire and water don't mix.

Puzzle/race game created for the Global Game Jam in January 2022. Goal of the game is to get to the end of the level as quickly as possible.

My role: Programming follower behaviour. Level/puzzle design.

Tools: Unity, C#

Github: github.com/HeavenlyAwe/fgj22.

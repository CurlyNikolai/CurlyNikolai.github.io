They Are You

They Are You

Become Who You Kill

Summary

Killed by your father, resurrected by the God of Death, granted the ability to possess the bodies of your fallen enemies, embark on an epic of revenge. Escape the halls of the underworld and ascend the ancient tower at the top of which your father abodes. Use your reactiveness and wit to clear room after room in this babylovecraftian environment.

Puzzle/action game created for the Game Project course at Aalto university. The objective of the game is to get through each room filled with enemies presenting a puzzly challenge.

My role: Programming, AI behaviour, designer tools, pitching.

Tools: Unity, C#

Build: aalto-gamedesign.itch.io/they-are-you.

Github: github.com/oshirokn/They-Are-You_HDRP.

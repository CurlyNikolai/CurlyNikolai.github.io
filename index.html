About Me

Hi, I'm Emil.

I'm a twenty-something year old computational physicist turned game programmer, living in Helsinki, Finland.

Currently I study game design at Aalto University on the programming side. Before that I worked as a computational physicist, researching nuclear reactor materials. My main interests are gameplay programming and graphics, but I'm also interested in the design and narrative aspect of games.

Besides all that, I'm a dude who likes to play games, go to the gym, and socialize. I also have a healthy obsession with kitchen gardening and baking bread. Oh, and did I tell you I have a dream of founding a winery and writing a book? Anyway.

Below in the projects section you can find some stuff that I've done and feel comfortable enough to share online. Please, have a look! Hope you find something you like.


Game Engines: Mainly Unity. A little Unreal Engine.

Programming Languages: Python, C#, C++, Bash, Fortran
